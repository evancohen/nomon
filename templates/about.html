A bit about us...

NomON is a quirky new mobile app that makes ordering delivery food crazy easy. We take the decision cost out of picking what, where and when to eat - and add in a fun twist.

How does it work?

You tell us where you are, pick a price, identify your allergies- and here's the kicker- the meal that's delivered to you is totally random! Kind of crazy, sort of a gamble, but that's the fun part! After you get your order, we'll ask you whether or not you liked it- that way, we learn what you don't like while still keeping your orders random.

We're a team of kooky college kids, and we built this in a weekend. We're still working on making it perfect, so "like" us to keep up with us. We're planning to launch fully functional in the next few weeks!
