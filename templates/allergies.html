*nomON and ordr.in do not guarantee that the meal you receive will match your allergy preferences. The nomON algorithm selects and avoids items according to the ingredients that each restaurant lists on its menu. Thus, if a restaurant does not disclose the full ingredient list, there is no guarantee that your order will not include things you specified. We suggest only selecting certain categories of restaurants that will serve your needs, thoroughly inspecting foods when received, or refraining from ordering if your allergies are severe.
